Random Topics

Cloud Watching

Clouds are free abstract art. Some look like mountains, some look like soup, and some look like the sky forgot what it was drawing halfway through.

Snack Logic

The best snack is usually the one you did not plan. A random biscuit, cold fruit, or leftover slice of pizza can feel like treasure.

Lost Objects

Keys and chargers have a talent for hiding in obvious places. They wait until you check the impossible places first, then reappear beside your hand.

Background Music

Lo-fi beats, game soundtracks, rain noise, and movie scores all change the same room into completely different places.
